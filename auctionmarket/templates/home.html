Welcome to the Bolt Road

Welcome to our micromobility startup's web application where you can bid on high-quality electric bicycles, skateboards, hoverboards, and scooters in a fun and exciting auction format. What's unique about our platform is that all the proceeds from the auctions go directly to charity.

Our goal is to make a positive impact on the world by not only providing eco-friendly transportation options but also by supporting charitable causes that help those in need. By participating in our auctions, you not only have the opportunity to purchase top-of-the-line electric micromobility devices, but you also contribute to making a difference in the world.

We believe that everyone should have access to eco-friendly transportation options and that we can all make a positive impact on the world. That's why we're proud to offer a fun and engaging way to support charitable causes while also acquiring the latest electric micromobility devices on the market.

In addition to our commitment to supporting charitable causes, we also value the security and transparency of our auction process. That's why we utilize the Ethereum Goerli blockchain to save auction outcomes, ensuring that the ownership of the micromobility devices you purchase is fully documented and verified. By using this technology, we can guarantee that you are the true owner of your new eco-friendly ride and that your purchase is backed by a secure and decentralized system. Join us in our efforts to make a positive impact on the world, while also enjoying the benefits of cutting-edge technology and eco-friendly transportation options.

To ensure a fair and transparent bidding process, we have established the following rules:

- You can bid any amount you want, as long as it is greater than the minimum bid set by us and the current highest bid. This allows you to make competitive bids for the micromobility devices you want.

- You can withdraw your bid at any time if you change your mind. However, please note that the withdrawal of a bid does not revive the previous one. Instead, the auction will continue with the next highest bidder, so make sure you are committed to your bid before placing it.

- Once the auction has ended, the micromobility device will be removed from the auction market, and the outcome will be recorded in the Ethereum Goerli blockchain. This ensures the validity and transparency of the auction process. You can verify the auction outcome directly by clicking on the tx ID provided in your profile page.

We want to provide a fun and engaging way to support charitable causes while acquiring the latest electric micromobility devices on the market. Our goal is to make the world a better place, one ride at a time. Thank you for joining us in our mission, and happy bidding!
